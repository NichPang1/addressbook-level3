Health Booklet

This is a alternative to the primitive pen and paper management system for hospitals to store patient data as well as doctor information and their schedule. Doctors will be able to have essential patients information literally at their fingertips! In accordance with PDPA, doctors will only be able to see patients information that are relevant to them!

Features * High level security (Authentication and Authorization) * Essential and relevant patients' information with just a click * Check your coworkers schedule * Undo/Redo - no need to fret over accidentally deleting your patients' information * Online integrated communication
